Avoid
Alcohol — kills recovery, disrupts sleep, spikes cortisol. Even weekends.
Sugary drinks — Lucozade, Ribena, fizzy drinks, fruit juice. Empty calories with no protein.
Ultra-processed food — crisps, biscuits, fast food, ready meals. Inflammatory, nutrient-empty.
White bread and pastries — spike blood sugar and crash energy fast. Switch to wholegrain.
Excess dairy — cheese is fine in the portions shown. Don't pour milk on everything.
Late-night eating — eating after 9pm when you're trying to lean out disrupts fat burning during sleep.
Coffee and green tea are fine. 2–3L water daily is non-negotiable.

Progression
Phase 1 — Weeks 1–3: Foundation
3 sets per exercise. Learn ring stability and movement patterns. 90 sec rest. Fill tins to 5–8kg.
Phase 2 — Weeks 4–6: Build
4 sets on all main lifts. Load push-ups with 8kg KB on back. Fill tins to 12–15kg. Reduce rest to 75 sec.
Phase 3 — Weeks 7–9: Intensity
Archer push-ups, pistol squat negatives, ring muscle-up progressions. Rest 60 sec. Add 3s eccentric tempo.
Phase 4 — Weeks 10–13: Peak
Max reps, supersets throughout. 3s eccentric on everything. Fill tins to 20–25kg for carries and squats.
Kit progression
Rings: rows → dips → push-ups → tuck front lever → muscle-up transition
Tins: 5kg carries → 10kg rows → 15kg goblet squats → 20kg+ farmer carries
Kettlebell: ballast for push-ups · rows · swings · one-arm overhead press
Bench (if you get one): dips · incline push-ups · hip thrusts · step-ups
